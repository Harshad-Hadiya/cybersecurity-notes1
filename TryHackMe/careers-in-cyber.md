## Room Name
Careers in Cyber

## What I Learned
Cybersecurity has multiple career paths
Each role has different responsibilities
Both offensive and defensive security are important

## Cyber Security Careers
# Penetration Tester (Ethical Hacker)
Finds vulnerabilities in systems by simulating real attacks in a legal environment.

 # SOC Analyst
Monitors security alerts and detects potential cyber attacks in real time.

# Security Engineer
Designs, builds, and maintains secure systems and infrastructure.

# Digital Forensics Analyst
Investigates cyber incidents and collects digital evidence after attacks.

#  Malware Analyst
Analyzes malicious software to understand its behavior and impact.

# SOC (Security Operations Center)
A team that monitors systems 24/7, detects threats, and responds to security incidents.

# Threat Intelligence
Collecting and analyzing information about potential cyber threats and attackers.

#  DFIR (Digital Forensics & Incident Response)
Process of investigating cyber attacks and responding with proper actions.

# SIEM
A system that collects and analyzes logs to detect suspicious activities and security threats.

##  Offensive vs Defensive Security
Offensive Security = Finding weaknesses in systems (ethical hacking)
Defensive Security = Protecting systems and detecting attacks

## Key Learning
Cybersecurity has multiple career paths, and each role plays an important part in securing systems and organizations.
