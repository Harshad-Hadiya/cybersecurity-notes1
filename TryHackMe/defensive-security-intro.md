## Key Concepts

# SOC (Security Operations Center)
Team that monitors and detects cyber attacks in real time.
# Threat Intelligence
Information about potential cyber threats and attackers.
# DFIR
Process of investigating cyber incidents and collecting digital evidence.
# Malware Analysis
Studying malicious software to understand its behavior.
# SIEM
Tool that collects and analyzes logs from systems for security monitoring.
